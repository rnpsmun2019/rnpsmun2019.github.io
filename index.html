About R-NPSMUN

NPS Rajajinagar proudly presents to you the third edition of R-NPSMUN in 2019!!!

An amalgamation of the consistent committees fused with a few unconventional ones, we have ensured that each delegate, new or experienced, has something to look forward to. Delegates will have to think on their feet to keep up with the pace of the debate, combating the obstacles posed, while sticking to the portfolios represented. Alliances will be made, strategies devised, while keeping the nations’ best interests at heart. Factual knowledge along with rational arbitrations will sail you through!

So delegates, come be a part of this exhilarating experience!
