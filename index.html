ABOUT R-NPSMUN




NPS Rajajinagar proudly presents the third edition of R-NPSMUN in 2019!!! An amalgamation of consistent committees fused with a few unconventional ones, we have ensured that each delegate, new or experienced, has something to look forward to. Delegates will have to think on their feet to keep up with the pace of the debate, combating the obstacles posed, while sticking to the portfolios represented. Alliances will be made, strategies devised, while keeping the nations’ best interests at heart. Factual knowledge along with rational arbitrations will sail you through! So delegates, come be a part of this exhilarating experience!

LETTER FROM THE SECRETARY GENERAL

Letter from the Secretary General


Dear delegates,

On behalf of the Executive Board of all the committees, I am extremely honoured and privileged to welcome you all to RNPSMUN 2019.

This MUN seeks to bring out the best in each delegate in terms of both assertiveness and diplomacy and by its end, I guarantee, you all will learn more than you ever expected and become better speakers, armed with tact and poise. Though being a delegate may seem a daunting task, I assure you it will not be without a dash of fun and excitement. Along the way you’ll have a clearer perception on the issues that plague our world today.

This edition comes with the promise of five intriguing committees which will , without a doubt, give you an exhilarating experience. The problems are myriad, and the solutions, never binary, each one a paradox in itself. The New World Order, a prognostication of the transient world , set in a completely different timeline, a problem which the delegates of the Futuristic Security Council must ponder upon and come up with avant garde solutions but there’s a catch: you never know what’s going to happen. While the future may seem a question, the past poses a bigger one in the historic Indian Parliament, staged by the Council of Lahore (CoLa) . History will take a different turn and delegates will no longer be just mute spectators. The scientifically inclined will definitely be enamoured by the International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO). After all, the geek shall inherit the Earth and in this case save it as well. The truth will set you free and the Press Corps will let you see. The journalists will be the voice of reason and raise pertinent questions in the nerve wracking press conferences. As a finale, I present to you the INTERPOL. Dealing with the Thieves in law, an enigma wrapped in a mystery, no crime will go unpunished in this committee where ‘good cop’ gains a whole new meaning.

From being a nervous first timer three years back to being a Secretary General now, the journey seems surreal and I hope you will enjoy MUNning as much as I do. Here’s hoping to see all of you in the conference this year!
Sincerely,
Srijan Chakraborty
Secretary General
