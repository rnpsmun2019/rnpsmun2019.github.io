International Press Corps

“We have to uphold a free press and freedom of speech. Because, in the end, lies and misinformation are no match for the truth”
~ Barack Obama

A conglomerate of the major new agencies of the world, The International Press Corps has always played a key role in arbitrating inter-governmental and international conflicts. The International Press acts as the bridge between the proceedings at global forums and the public, making them an indispensable aspect of world politics. The Press is also responsible for keeping multinational organisations accountable and shaping public opinion. The R-NPSMUN offers a platform to all aspiring journalists, to scrutinise and analyse the working of the committees, the efficiency of the delegates and their understanding of their countries’ political stance. Journalists will have to realise the power at hand, and use it to their discretion, to ensure that the delegates have a command over their debate in committee. We welcome all delegates who believe in making a difference, for although it is cliché, the pen is mightier than the sword!
