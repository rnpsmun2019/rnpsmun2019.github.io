INTERNATIONAL PRESS CORPS




As a member of the IPC, you will have to represent a national or international news agency instead of a country. In preparation for this role, you must write an editorial instead of a position paper. During the conference, the reporters will inform the conference about the happenings in the committees through the Press Conferences. It is engaging work, and more importantly it is fun.

Dear Delegates,

Welcome to RMUN 2019!! We are extremely honored and excited to serve as the council for the International Press Corps.

The press has the privilege and responsibility of keeping the public informed and keeping those in power accountable! Over the course of the two days, you will assume the role of an international news agency and cover the events of the conference through that perspective.

Each news organization is unique in its tone, audience, slant, and editorial stance. As a journalist, you will be breaking stories, holding delegates accountable to their positions, communicating breakthroughs to the entire conference, along with showing your flair in writing and eye for minute details. You will get to interview delegates, write news articles, press release briefs and opinion pieces.

The role of the Press Corps at RMUN 2019 and in the workings of international democracy cannot be understated. We hope that the first-hand experiences you will get as a journalist will further instill in you the importance of a free and independent press.

We are here as an expedient to you while you prepare for the committee and beyond. Please reach out to us with any questions or concerns you might have - even if it’s just getting to know each other better!

We look forward to meeting you all and hope you have fun at this year’s conference!



Prajval Sairam Krishnan (Head of Press)
Sushanth Shenoy (Director of Press)
