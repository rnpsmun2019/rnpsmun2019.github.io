FUTURISTIC SECURITY COUNCIL




"Remain not oblivious to probable oblivion"

A committee that demands critical thinking and riveting debate, the Security Council is indispensable for the maintenance of international peace and security. It is a committee best equipped to deal with the pressing issues that plague our transient world. The Security Council is the only body of the United Nations that can enforce resolutions upon other member states. It handles sensitive subjects, each of which are as microscopic as they are macroscopic. Look closely and you’ll see, reality becoming sharper, but when you take a step back the future fades. Committed to taking action to shield nations against genocide, war crimes, ethnic cleansing, cyber terrorism and crimes against humanity, the Security Council is well known for the quick decisions it takes in times of crisis. To succeed in this committee, a holistic understanding about foreign policy is imperative along with immaculate attention to detail. This committee will move forward with as much order as chaos, maybe meandering but purposefully so.

Address from the Executive Board


Delegates!

Welcome to the Futuristic Security Council, a continuous crisis committee! Throughout the course of the 2 days, we guarantee heavy and engaging debate. Breaches of loyalty, conspiracies, and scrutinized surveillance may or may not be possible. Don’t let your illusions and presumptions overtake your stealth. You shall witness the military prowess of your country and your negotiating skills tested to the maximum. Knowledge about your military resources, stances and foreign policy as well as directives, is a compulsion. To withhold the enigma of the committee, we refrain from exposing too much. We have an incredible set of crisis updates lined up for you that is sure to exercise your brain and compel you to find solutions to probable future problems. We guarantee a stimulating experience as we know what it takes to be a delegate in a Security Council. This is an opportunity for you, delegates, to prevent a New World Order! Sit back and enjoy. Because no matter what, we’ll be 3 steps, 5 steps, 7 steps ahead of you. And just when you think you’re catching up, that’s when we’ll be right behind you.
